"Semantic Non-Fungibility and Violations of the Law of One Price in Prediction Markets" — Gebele & Matthes (TUM) · Jan 2026

First human-validated cross-venue dataset of 100k+ aligned prediction markets across 10 platforms (2018-2025). Finds **persistent 2-4% execution-aware price deviations between semantically equivalent markets**, even in liquid settings — driven by structural frictions, not info disagreement. Direct empirical baseline for hermes-arb's edge thesis (Kalshi↔Polymarket 5-min BTC). The semantic-alignment framework (NL description + resolution semantics + temporal scope) is the same matching problem hermes-arb must solve to map Kalshi range → PM binary.
