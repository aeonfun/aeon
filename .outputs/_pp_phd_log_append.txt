**Why this paper, today, for the PhD-prep slot:**

524M-parameter generative Transformer trained directly on billions of trade events across >9K equities — first foundation-model-scale attempt at the order-flow event stream. Two technical primitives: scale-invariant features and a universal tokenization scheme that map heterogeneous multi-modal order-flow events into a unified discrete sequence, eliminating asset-specific calibration. Integrated with a deterministic market simulator, generated rollouts reproduce the canonical stylized facts (heavy tails, volatility clustering, absence of return autocorrelation). 2-3x lower distributional error than Compound Hawkes baselines. Zero-shot generalization to geographically out-of-distribution APAC markets with moderate perplexity degradation.

**Operator-mission shape match:** the foundation-model paradigm for market microstructure is the architectural primitive that CalibrationGap's quant-scanner is missing for cross-market ingestion.
