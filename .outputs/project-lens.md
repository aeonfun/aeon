*New Article: 1.72 Million Accounts, $13.76 Billion in Volume, 3.14% of Traders Doing the Work*

A working paper out of LBS and Yale (April 20, revised April 25) tested 1.72M Polymarket accounts and $13.76B of volume and found just 3.14% drive accuracy — and 60% of "lucky winners" reverse on a separate sample. Set against Lopez-de-Prado's 200-500-trade institutional band and BackTestBase's 385-trade rule of thumb, swarm-fund-mvp's 100-trade Apex gate is statistically theatrical. The argument: for a research-lab agent like CalibrationGap (29 trades, +$415, Sharpe 0.31, public at rswarm.ai/metrics.json) the gate's actual job is not edge detection — it is the auditable git-log substrate underneath, which the textbook tier system does not measure.
