*prediction markets — 2026-05-07*

_TL;DR: SEC freezes 24 prediction-market ETF launches, Roobet plugs prediction trading into a major crypto casino, and Kalshi clears its first institutional block trade — financial plumbing is outpacing the regulatory framework._

1. *SEC pauses 24 prediction-market ETFs days before Roundhill's May-7 launch*
   The SEC invoked its 75-day extension on event-contract ETFs from Roundhill, Bitwise, and GraniteShares, freezing the retail-wrapper category before any product traded. Roundhill's first fund had been scheduled to launch May 7. No redo deadline; the SEC said it is "actively engaged" with sponsors.
   Why it matters: ETF-mediated retail flow is blocked indefinitely; Kalshi and Polymarket's $20B+ funding talks priced this rail in.
   https://bettorsinsider.com/news/2026/05/06/the-sec-just-paused-more-than-two-dozen-prediction-market-etfs-what-it-means-for-kalshi-and-polymarket/

2. *Roobet launches prediction markets — first major crypto casino to integrate the format*
   Roobet, a licensed crypto casino founded 2019, opened roobet.com/predictions on May 6 with sports, culture, and macro contracts settled inside existing user balances. No new account, no new wallet.
   Why it matters: Polymarket competitors have shipped new venues; Roobet ships event contracts as a feature inside a sportsbook with KYC'd, funded users — the cheapest path to scale yet attempted.
   https://www.benzinga.com/pressreleases/26/05/52332077/roobet-launches-prediction-markets-on-may-6-the-first-major-crypto-casino-to-integrate-the-format

3. *Kalshi clears its first institutional block trade — Jump and a Houston enviro fund on California carbon*
   A May-4 Bernstein note flagged Kalshi's first bespoke block, on the California May carbon auction clearing price. Jump Trading made the market; Greenlight Commodities brokered for a Houston environmental hedge fund. Size undisclosed.
   Why it matters: bespoke blocks are how flow that won't fit screen liquidity gets absorbed — first concrete evidence prediction markets can carry non-retail size.
   https://crypto.news/prediction-markets-enter-institutional-era-after-first-kalshi-block-trade/
