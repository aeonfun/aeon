2. *Nevada judge grants preliminary injunction; Kalshi must geofence sports, elections, entertainment by May 4*

   Why it matters: first US state to land a hard-edge geofencing order against a CFTC designated contract market — the template state regulators in Utah, Minnesota and Massachusetts will copy if their statutes stall in legislature.
