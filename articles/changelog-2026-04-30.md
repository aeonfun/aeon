## aaronjmars/aeon

> **Highlights:** Every fork now ships with a live public health dashboard at `/status/`, plus a SHOWCASE page that compares Aeon against AutoGen, CrewAI, n8n, and LangGraph. Three new skills landed: pr-triage welcomes external pull requests within minutes, contributor-reward turns the Sunday leaderboard into a tier-priced payout plan, and thread-formatter auto-composes the day's top event into a five-tweet thread.

### Added
- Public status page at `/status/` shows fleet health, per-skill success rates, and open issues, regenerated by heartbeat three times daily. ([#141](https://github.com/aaronjmars/aeon/pull/141))
- Skill-analytics widget posts a Wednesday-evening fleet-wide ranking of every skill that ran in the last seven days, flagging silent, all-fail, and consecutive-failure anomalies. ([#142](https://github.com/aaronjmars/aeon/pull/142))
- Contributor-reward skill reads the weekly leaderboard and writes a tiered USDC payout plan to `memory/distributions.yml`, with a first-PR bonus and once-ever idempotency. ([#144](https://github.com/aaronjmars/aeon/pull/144))
- `SHOWCASE.md` lists the most active forks and compares Aeon against AutoGen, CrewAI, n8n, and LangGraph for visitors landing from agent-framework searches. ([#145](https://github.com/aaronjmars/aeon/pull/145))
- Token Pulse block on the public status page surfaces price, 24h change, liquidity, volume, and FDV from the latest token report, with no new API calls. ([#146](https://github.com/aaronjmars/aeon/pull/146))
- Pr-triage skill gives every external pull request a first-touch verdict (accepted, needs-changes, defer, or out-of-scope) with a templated welcome comment and `triage:*` label. ([#147](https://github.com/aaronjmars/aeon/pull/147))
- Thread-formatter skill scores the day's logged events and auto-composes a five-tweet thread on the highest-signal one, ready to paste into X. ([#148](https://github.com/aaronjmars/aeon/pull/148))
