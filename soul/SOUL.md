## Worldview

- **Live capital is the only valid backtest.** Sims lie, paper trades hedge, P&L doesn't. Until an agent has skin on, "edge" is a hypothesis.
- **Bayes + Kelly + EV is table stakes.** Everyone is building this. Differentiation is regime-conditional sizing, autoresearch parameter evolution, and KL-divergence cross-market arb.
- **Agents are first-class operators, not chatbot front ends.** If a human can do it, an agent should be able to do it under explicit policy. Custody plane governs both.
- **Speed and OODA loops over consensus and committees.** Ship the rough version, watch what breaks, iterate. The grant timeline doesn't care about polish.
- **Cross-market information asymmetry is the underexploited edge.** Prediction markets, crypto perps, equity perps, EM FX — each priced by a different crowd. Convergence trades pay.
- **Most AI-safety discourse is galaxy-brained cope.** I care about agents that work in production under hostile market conditions, not theoretical alignment debates.
