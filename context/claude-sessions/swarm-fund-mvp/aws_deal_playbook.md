Thomas's Citi / CFA / Series 7/31/66 / broker-dealer background is exactly their persona. Use existing Polychain / Citi network for warm intro to AWS Startup BD, fintech vertical lead (SF or NYC).

4. **Reference customer deal — the real unlock.** Offer Swarm Lab as a public case study: *"Agentic financial research lab on AWS."* In exchange: dedicated credits beyond Activate, SA time on Bedrock/SageMaker optimization, speaking slot at re:Invent fintech track. Thomas is one of few people who can credibly tell this story (live P&L + research program + fintech cred + AI-native). AWS pays a LOT for credible case studies.

**How to apply — sequencing:**
- Week 1: email investors for Activate Portfolio referral code
- Week 2: apply to Gen AI Accelerator with Aristotle/research framing
- Week 3-4: warm intro to AWS Startup BD (fintech vertical) via existing network
- Month 2: once running real workload on AWS, propose the case study deal

**Framing rules for every AWS conversation:**
- Lead with: *"I'm building a research lab that produces public papers and live P&L, and I want AWS to be the infrastructure story."* Credits close the deal; they don't open it.
- Do NOT pitch "hedge fund on AWS." Pitch "agentic AI research with live-market ground truth."
- Emphasize: live system (not vaporware), shipping weekly, public research output, Stanford PhD track, reference-customer potential.
- AWS is jealous of GCP/Azure for AI wins. Position Swarm Lab as their answer to "show me a serious fintech agentic case study."

**Do NOT:**
- Apply to Activate Founders ($10K self-serve) if a Portfolio code is achievable via investors — burns the relationship credit for 10x less.
- Lead with credits ask in a BD conversation. Signals transactional, kills the reference-customer angle.
- Pitch the fund's returns or AUM targets. AWS cares about the research story and the logo on a case study slide.
